App Description

SuperbAI revolutionizes how you access and manage information with its seamless integration of AI and Vision capabilities. The app provides an effortless experience by allowing you to interact with content directly from your screen without switching between multiple apps. With the floating button feature, you can easily activate voice commands, access a search bar, or view floating displays for quick information retrieval. Smart Vision integration lets you capture screenshots or photos and instantly get insights or answers about what’s on your screen. Customize your experience with various AI roles tailored to different needs, and use simple voice commands or the search bar to execute tasks, browse, and manage contacts. Automated reminders ensure you never miss a task or meeting. SuperbAI offers intelligent, on-demand information access, making it an essential tool for modern convenience. For more details, simply tap the 'Info' button within the app.

Testing Instructions

  1. Installation and Setup:
      Download and install the SuperbAI app
    • Download and install the SuperbAI app from the designated app store.
    • Grant the app 'All Files Access' permission to enable auto-detection of screenshots and camera photos.
  2. Home Screen:
    • Open the app and navigate to the Home Screen.
    • Verify that you can select your AI’s Role and Language from the dropdown menu.
    • Test each AI role to ensure it performs as described and displays its unique strengths.
  3. Anywhere Access:
    • Ensure the floating button is visible on all screens.
    • Long Press Gesture:
      • Long-press the floating button to activate Speech Recognition.
      • Ask various questions (e.g., "What is Neural Network?") and verify that results are displayed as a floating message.
    • Toss to Activate Search Bar:
      • Toss the floating button to enable the Search Bar.
      • Double-tap the search bar to access 'VIVA-Commands' and test commands such as 'search what is AI?', 'show me contacts', 'show me dial/number pad', 'set alarm at 5 PM', and 'show meetings/tasks'.
    • Double Tap Gesture:
      • Double-tap the floating button to activate speech recognition.
      • Test voice commands for browsing, calling, and setting alarms to ensure they function as expected.
  4. Vision Integration:
    • Take a screenshot and verify that you are redirected to VIVA-Vision.
    • Ask questions related to the screenshot (e.g., "What is this? Tell me more about it!").
    • Take a photo using the camera and ask questions about it using VIVA-Vision.
  5. Permissions:
    • Confirm that the app requests and is granted 'All Files Access' permission for auto-detection features to work correctly.
  6. Info Button:
    • Locate and use the 'Info' button on various screens to ensure it provides accurate details about the app.
  7. General Usability:
    • Navigate through the app to check for ease of use, responsiveness, and accuracy of the features.
    • Report any issues or inconsistencies encountered during testing.
